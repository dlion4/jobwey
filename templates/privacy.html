Privacy Policy for Jobwey Job Agency

*Last Updated: 31st August 2023*

At Jobwey, we value your trust and are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you visit our website, engage with our services, or interact with us.

1. Information We Collect:

1.1 Personal Information:

When you register on our platform or submit an application, we may collect personal information, including but not limited to your name, contact details, resume, cover letter, and other relevant documents.

1.2 Job Postings and Employer Information:

For employers using our services to post job opportunities, we may collect information about your company, job descriptions, and other details necessary for recruitment.

1.3 Usage Data:

We gather information about how you interact with our website and services. This may include your IP address, browser type, device information, and pages visited.

1.4 Cookies:

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. Cookies are small data files stored on your device that help us recognize your preferences and improve our services.

2. How We Use Your Information:

2.1 Recruitment Process:

Your personal information is used to facilitate the recruitment process. This includes matching candidates with suitable job opportunities, sharing your profile with potential employers, and arranging interviews.

2.2 Communication:

We may use your contact details to communicate with you regarding job opportunities, application status, and other relevant updates. You can manage your communication preferences in your account settings.

2.3 Service Improvement:

Analyzing usage data helps us improve our website and services. We use this information to enhance user experience, optimize job matching, and provide personalized content.

3. Data Security:

3.1 Confidentiality:

We prioritize the confidentiality of your information. Access to personal data is restricted to authorized personnel involved in the recruitment process.

3.2 Security Measures:

We employ industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction. This includes encryption, secure server infrastructure, and regular security audits.

4. Third-Party Services:

4.1 Job Postings:

To provide a comprehensive job listing, we may integrate with third-party job boards. Please be aware that these platforms have their privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

4.2 Analytics and Marketing:

We use third-party analytics and marketing tools to better understand user behavior and preferences. These tools may use cookies and other tracking technologies.

5. Your Choices:

5.1 Account Settings:

You have the option to review and update your account information, including personal details and communication preferences.

5.2 Opt-Out:

If you no longer wish to receive communications from us, you can opt-out through the unsubscribe link provided in our emails or by contacting our support team.

6. Retention and Deletion:

6.1 Data Retention:

We retain your personal information for the duration necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy. After this period, we securely delete or anonymize your data.

6.2 Account Deactivation:

You can request the deactivation of your account by contacting us. Upon account deactivation, your personal information will be securely archived or deleted.

7. International Data Transfers:

As a recruitment agency dealing with foreign labor, your data may be transferred and stored internationally. We ensure that such transfers comply with applicable data protection laws.

8. Updates to Privacy Policy:

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of significant updates, and the latest version will be available on our website.

9. Contact Us:

If you have questions or concerns about our Privacy Policy, please contact us at our Contact us Page. We are here to address any inquiries and ensure your privacy is respected.

By using Jobwey Recruitment Agency's services, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy. We appreciate your trust in us and are committed to safeguarding your privacy throughout your recruitment journey.

*Thank you for choosing Jobwey Job Agency!*
